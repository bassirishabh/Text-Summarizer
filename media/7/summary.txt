Cold and weary, the merchant had lost all hope of reaching an inn when he suddenly noticed a bright light shining in the middle of a wood.
Overcome by curiosity, he ventured upstairs, where the corridor led into magnificent rooms and halls.
When he woke next morning, an unknown hand had placed a mug of steaming coffee and some fruit by his bedside.
Two bloodshot eyes, gleaming angrily, glared at him and a deep, terrifying voice growled: "Ungrateful man!

Then it started to say a few kind words, till in the end, Beauty was amazed to discover that she was actually enjoying its conversation.
Beauty stayed beside him for hours on end, describing her life at the Castle, and explaining that the Beast was really good and kind.
Her heart in her mouth, Beauty ran into the garden and there crouched the Beast, its eyes shut, as though dead.
